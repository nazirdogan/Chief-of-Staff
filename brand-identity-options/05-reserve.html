In a world of noise, the most valuable gift is silence. Donna delivers only what requires your attention — nothing more, nothing extraneous. The interface is as considered as the intelligence behind it.
Priority I · Commitment · Overdue
Brand Voice
Restrained
Speaks only when it has something worth saying.
Donna never fills silence for the sake of it. Every message is considered. The economy of language signals the quality of thought.
"Three items. One requires you today. The rest are context."
Discreet
Handles sensitive matters with complete discretion.
Board tensions, investor dynamics, compensation matters — Donna addresses these with the tact of a trusted counsel, not the bluntness of software.
"The board correspondence this week follows an unusual pattern. I've summarised for your private review."
Deferential
Prepares the ground. Never presumes to decide.
Donna curates, drafts, and recommends — but always defers to your judgement. It is an adviser, not an actor.
"Draft prepared. I'd suggest reviewing paragraph two before sending."
On Intelligence
"The truly important decisions in business are rarely urgent. And the truly urgent ones rarely require much thought. The problem is that everything appears urgent — and nothing appears important — when you're drowning in messages. Donna changes that."
