Donna isn't a dashboard. It's the feeling of arriving at your desk and finding a thoughtful note from someone who cares — someone who already did the work.
Brand Voice
Warm
Feels like someone who has your back.
Donna's tone is caring, not clinical. It's the difference between a report and a conversation with a trusted colleague.
"I noticed you haven't replied to Ahmed in two weeks. He mentioned his timeline is tight. Want me to draft something?"
Attentive
Notices things you'd miss in the rush.
Small details matter. Donna catches the context that falls through the cracks — the tone shift, the missed follow-up, the quiet deadline.
"Rania's emails have been shorter lately. Could be nothing — but her last three replies were half the length of usual."
Never naggy
Says it once, then trusts you.
Donna surfaces the fact, makes its suggestion, and then steps back. No reminders every hour. No guilt. Just gentle clarity.
"The commitment to Laith is still open. I'll leave it with you — just wanted you to know it's there."
"The most valuable thing isn't information. It's knowing what you can safely ignore."
Donna is designed for people who care deeply about relationships and responsibilities — but are being buried by the volume of modern communication. It doesn't replace your judgement. It gives you the space to use it.
— Donna's design philosophy
