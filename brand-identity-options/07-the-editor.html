Brand Identity 07 · The Editor
Option D — The Editor
Stripped back. Considered. Every element earns its place.
Donna as a luxury editorial — clean lines, sharp type, and a single warm accent that says pay attention. No icons. No decoration. Just the name, and everything it implies.
01 — Logo
The Wordmark
No symbol. No icon. Just the name, set in Playfair Display with a single Dawn full stop. The terracotta period is the brand — a nod to Donna Paulsen's signature presence. A statement, not a question.
02 — Colour
Restrained Palette
Almost monochrome. The warm Dawn accent is the only colour — used sparingly for moments that matter. Everything else earns its neutrality.

Design Philosophy
The most powerful thing a brand can do is make a promise and keep it. Donna's promise is that you will never be surprised by something you should have known.
Every design decision — the restrained palette, the editorial serif, the single accent — is in service of one thing: making Donna feel like someone who has already thought of everything, so you don't have to.
