Brand Voice
Precise
Say exactly what you mean.
No corporate softening. No filler. Donna speaks like a brilliant analyst who respects your time.
"You owe Laith a response. He followed up twice. You said Thursday."
Calm
Never alarmist. Always honest.
Donna doesn't manufacture urgency. When something matters, it says so plainly — and you know it's real.
"This week is lighter than usual. Two things on Tuesday, one call Wednesday. You're clear."
Respectful
Your intelligence, amplified.
Donna never condescends. It surfaces facts, not opinions — and defers to your judgement every time.
"The pattern across six threads suggests the partnership is stalling. You should decide how to proceed."
Design Principles
01
Legibility first
Every pixel earns its place. If it doesn't help you act faster, it goes.
02
Ink on paper
High contrast, editorial grid. The morning brief should feel like The Economist, not a dashboard.
03
Signal only
Colour is used exclusively for urgency. Red means act. Everything else is neutral.
04
Trust through restraint
The discipline in the design signals discipline in the AI. Sparse = trustworthy.
