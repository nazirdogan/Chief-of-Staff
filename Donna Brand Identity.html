The name is a promise. If you know who Donna Paulsen is, you understand this product instantly. If you don't — you will.

01 — Brand Foundation
What donna is.
The core idea
Donna is your personal intelligence layer — a brilliantly discreet chief of staff who reads everything so you don't have to. Every morning, one focused briefing: what matters, what you promised, who's gone quiet, and what to do first. She acts only when you say so. She watches always.
What she is not
Donna is not a chatbot. She's not a to-do app. She doesn't wait to be asked. She is a proactive background intelligence system that surfaces signal from noise — automatically, every single morning, delivered to you before you've had your coffee. The distinction is everything.

Why Midnight?
The color of awareness — the quiet, vigilant blue-black of 3am when Donna is reading your inbox so you can sleep. It anchors the brand in reliability, intelligence, and depth. Not a cold navy. Not a sterile black. The color of a confident mind at rest.
Why Dawn?
The warm terracotta of 7:30am — the exact moment the briefing arrives. Dawn says: action, warmth, urgency without anxiety. It separates Donna from every cold-blue AI product on the market. It's human. It's the color of the first thing that matters today.
Why Paper?
Donna handles serious things seriously. Pure white reads as clinical, digital, throwaway. Paper is warm, considered, permanent — the color of a briefing that was curated for you, not generated at you. It's the off-white of a letter worth reading.

06 — Brand Voice
How she speaks.

Donna Paulsen never needed more than two words to walk into a room. "I'm Donna" did the rest. The app carries the same energy — precise, warm, and never in doubt.

Precise
verbosevague
Every word earns its place. Donna says what matters in as few words as possible. She never explains when she can show. She never hedges when she's sure.

She's intelligent but human. "Good morning, Naz" — not "User briefing ready." She knows your name, your patterns, the people in your life who matter.

hedginguncertain
She doesn't say "you might want to." She says "this matters" and "here's what to do." Bold without being pushy. Certain without being cold.

One briefing. Everything that matters. She doesn't repeat herself or send endless notifications. She arrives once, precisely, and says exactly what you need.
